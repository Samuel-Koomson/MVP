About

This project was inspired by one of our team members who had been selling vegetables to corporate people who did not have the time to go to the market on regular basis. He saw the hustle in simply trying to buy healthy vegetables, Thus this project was aimed at making it easy for corporate people to easily find and purchase their healthy vegetables to meet the nutritional needs of their families. This is a portfolio project for the software engineering training program by ALX-Africa which is certified by Holberton school. This project was executed within 3 weeks.
